FAQ

Your questions answered

1. What is FORESTAHN Perfume?

FORESTAHN Perfume is a premium line of fragrances inspired by the beauty and tranquility of nature. Each scent is crafted to evoke the fresh, calming essence of forests.

2. How do I place an order?

You can place an order directly through our website by adding your favorite products to the shopping cart and following the checkout process.

3. What is the return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for any unused and unopened products. Please contact our support team for assistance with returns.

4. How long does shipping take?

Shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within South Korea. International shipping times vary depending on the destination.

5. Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email to track the status of your delivery.
